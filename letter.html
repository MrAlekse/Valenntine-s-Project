You were the kindest, most understanding, and most loving person I’ve ever met. You were always there for me, even at my lowest. But when you were struggling, I wasn’t there the way I should have been. I put myself first, and that was selfish. I’m truly sorry. But my heart still cares for you, still thinks about you, still hopes for you.
